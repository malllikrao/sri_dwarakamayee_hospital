Orthopedics

Expert care for bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments.

Understanding Our Orthopedics Department

Orthopaedics is a critical branch of medicine specializing in the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of musculoskeletal conditions. This field addresses issues related to bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments. At Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals in Karimnagar, with access to state-of-the-art orthopaedic facilities and expertise from skilled orthopaedic surgeons, patients receive comprehensive care. Whether dealing with fractures, arthritis, or sports injuries, our orthopaedic department provides effective solutions to restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals has some of the best orthopaedic doctors in Karimnagar who can provide you with tailored treatment plans and better recovery outcomes.

Our State-of-the-Art Services

Our Orthopaedic services are designed to keep you moving freely from head to toe through customized treatment, reconditioning, and education. Thousands of patients are treated by us every year. Our team includes some of the most experienced interventional radiologists, neurosurgeons, and plastic surgeons, ensuring a multidisciplinary approach to complex cases.

Orthopedic Treatment We Provide:

  • Joint Replacement: We have an impeccable track record in joint replacements, with fewer complications and a significantly low infection rate. We use the latest and most advanced treatments for knee joint replacement and more, to restore mobility and help you get back to the activities you enjoyed before joint pain interfered. Hence, we are a renowned bone and joint hospital. Our expertise lies in replacement surgeries of the hip, knee, shoulder, and elbow. We have recently revolutionized joint replacement surgeries by procuring advanced technology which makes joint replacement more precise, less painful, and ensures early functional recovery.
  • Arthroscopy and Sports Medicine: With numerous rehabilitation sessions per year, we are one of the leading rehabilitation centers in the region. Integrating conventional and regenerative orthopaedics with sports science, we provide programs designed to make you a better athlete with a focus on preventing secondary injuries. Our team of orthopaedic surgeons specializes in managing ACL & PCL meniscal tears, hamstring strain, ankle sprain, tennis elbow, and shoulder injuries (rotator cuff tears, dislocation, acromioclavicular joint dislocation, and clavicle fractures).
  • Paediatric Orthopaedics: Paediatric orthopaedic care is one of our specialties. Our team is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate orthopaedic care for children. In addition to paediatric upper limb, lower limb, and spine surgeries, our experts devise individualized paediatric orthopaedic treatment plans supporting your child every step of the way.
  • Limb Reconstruction: Congenital anomalies, long-standing infections, neglected fractures, and badly united fractures may lead to limb deformities. We offer the most modern surgical procedures for treating limb deformities of all types. Our limb reconstruction surgery team consists of a multidisciplinary group of orthopaedic surgeons and microvascular surgeons. To manage post-surgery care, we have a team of expert physiotherapists and pain management specialists.
  • Foot And Ankle Surgery: Foot and Ankle Surgery can align broken bones, fuse painful joints, remove overgrown tissues, or repair a torn ligament or tendon. We specialize in orthopaedic procedures for treating conditions like ankle impingement, ankle injuries, Achilles rupture, bunions, congenital foot disorders, diabetic foot, and many more.
  • Hand And Wrist Surgery: Our team of surgeons specializes in hand and wrist orthopaedic surgery. They manage hand and wrist pain, and bone and soft tissue conditions by using the latest delicate surgery techniques. Our orthopaedic surgeons specialize in performing awake surgery using localized anesthesia. With our latest surgical advancements, we can minimize hospital stay and patients can return to work on the same day (Day Care Surgery). Our occupational therapists offer top-of-the-line orthopaedic care that will help you regain full function and resume normal activities.
  • Ortho Tumors: Our Orthopaedic Oncology specialists have expertise in treating bone tumors and cancers of bones, joints, and muscles. These include conditions like giant cell tumors, fibrous dysplasias, bony cysts, osteochondromas, enchondromas, cancers in muscles near the bones, and metastatic bone cancers. We have the amalgamation of the best of surgical, medical, and radiation oncology for orthopaedic tumor care. We use the latest interventions in oncology and are managing most bone cancers with great success rates. Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals provides affordable limb salvage surgeries for the masses to reconstruct defects resulting after tumor excision.
  • Spine Surgery: If you have back pain that limits your mobility, we are here for you. Whether you need care for a common injury or complex spinal surgery, our specialists can restore your mobility and improve your quality of life. Our emphasis is on all aspects of spinal surgery treatment, such as physiotherapy, ergonomics, lifestyle awareness, and surgery. We use the latest medical advancements for back and spine surgery to ensure the best possible outcomes. Our dedicated team is committed to providing personalized care to help you achieve an active life free of spinal pain.
  • Shoulder Surgery: Shoulder surgery encompasses a range of procedures aimed at addressing various shoulder issues. An orthopaedic surgeon (a shoulder surgery specialist) can provide expert care, whether through minimally invasive shoulder surgery techniques, such as shoulder arthroscopy, or more extensive procedures like shoulder replacement surgery. These advanced methods are designed to reduce recovery time, minimize discomfort, and restore full shoulder function. Trust a specialist to guide you through the best surgical options for optimal outcomes.
