Understanding Our Dermatology Department

Dermatology plays a crucial role in maintaining healthy skin, hair, and nails, addressing a wide range of concerns from acne, eczema, and psoriasis to pigmentation disorders and hair loss. Expert Skin Specialists provide personalized care, leveraging advanced technology to diagnose and treat conditions effectively. Cosmetic dermatology, including chemical peels, laser therapy, and anti-ageing treatments, helps improve skin texture and appearance, boosting confidence. Specialized care for chronic conditions like psoriasis and eczema ensures long-term management, while targeted treatments for scars, wrinkles, and pigmentation deliver visible results. Whether for medical or aesthetic needs, dermatology services offer solutions tailored to individual concerns.

Skin Conditions We Treat & Procedures We Offer:

  • Chemical Peels: Chemical peels rejuvenate the skin by exfoliating the outer layer, improving texture, reducing pigmentation, and treating acne scars. This treatment enhances skin clarity and radiance for a youthful appearance.
  • Injectables (Botox & Fillers): Botox smoothens fine lines and wrinkles, while dermal fillers restore lost volume and enhance facial contours. These non-surgical treatments provide a refreshed, youthful look.
  • Lasers: Laser treatments effectively address pigmentation, scars, and unwanted hair. They offer precision and long-lasting results, improving skin texture and tone.
  • Microneedling RF: Microneedling RF stimulates collagen production to tighten skin and reduce scars, wrinkles, and stretch marks. It’s a minimally invasive treatment for skin rejuvenation.
  • Dermatosurgery: Dermatosurgery involves minor skin surgeries to treat lesions, cysts, or warts, ensuring functional and cosmetic improvement with minimal scarring.
  • Cosmetic Procedures: Cosmetic dermatology includes treatments like facelifts, skin tightening, and anti-ageing therapies, enhancing aesthetic appeal and boosting confidence.
  • Microblading for Eyebrows: Microblading reshapes and fills sparse eyebrows with semi-permanent pigment, delivering natural, fuller, and perfectly shaped brows.
  • Scalp Micropigmentation: Scalp micropigmentation replicates hair follicles using pigment, creating the appearance of a fuller scalp for individuals with hair thinning or baldness.
  • BB Glow and Lip Micropigmentation/Blush: BB Glow enhances skin tone and provides a semi-permanent foundation effect, while lip micropigmentation adds natural color for youthful, defined lips.
  • Vitiligo Surgery: Vitiligo surgery involves skin grafts or cellular transplantation to restore pigmentation in affected areas, improving skin tone consistency.
  • Acne Surgery: Acne surgery removes stubborn acne cysts and nodules, reducing inflammation and preventing scarring for clearer skin.
  • Scar Revision: Scar revision treatments minimize the appearance of scars from injuries, surgeries, or acne, improving skin texture and uniformity.
  • Ear Lobe Repair: Ear lobe repair corrects tears, stretching, or deformities caused by trauma or heavy earrings, restoring natural appearance and function.
  • Keloid Surgery: Keloid surgery removes raised scars and prevents regrowth with advanced techniques, providing smoother skin and improved aesthetics.
  • Lipoma and Sebaceous Cyst Excision: Lipoma and sebaceous cyst excision safely remove benign lumps, ensuring comfort and preventing further complications.
  • Mole Excision: Mole excision removes unwanted or suspicious moles for cosmetic or medical reasons, with minimal scarring and precision care.
  • Hair Transplant: Hair transplant restores natural hair growth by transferring healthy hair follicles to thinning or bald areas, offering long-lasting results.
