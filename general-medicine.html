General Medicine

Comprehensive care for common illnesses and chronic conditions.

Understanding Our General Medicine Department

The Department of General Medicine at Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals in Karimnagar deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and non-surgical treatment of various diseases. Our general physicians conduct thorough physical examinations and recommend necessary tests and diagnostic procedures. The results of these tests are then correlated to arrive at a precise diagnosis. Depending on the final diagnosis, appropriate treatment, medication, or referral for further specialized care is provided.

Why Choose Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals for General Medicine Care?

Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals' General Medicine Division in Karimnagar delivers integrated, multidisciplinary healthcare for a wide variety of medical conditions. Our 24/7 emergency team rapidly addresses critical issues to stabilize patients, followed by thorough evaluation and treatment. From common infections to complex diseases such as hypertension, diabetes, stroke, and heart ailments, we offer a full spectrum of general medicine care, along with expert internal medicine doctors who ensure each patient receives personalized attention.

Key Specialities within General Medicine:

  • Cardiology: Our cardiology unit manages coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure. Equipped for procedures like coronary angiography, echocardiography, treadmill tests, and revascularisation, we also provide Holter monitoring, EPS studies, and pacemaker implantations.
  • Endocrinology: We treat disorders related to hormones and metabolism, including thyroid dysfunction, diabetes, and adrenal or pituitary abnormalities. Care extends to paediatric and adult patients, ensuring personalized therapy for each individualâ€™s needs.
  • Nephrology: Our kidney care services encompass hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis. Renal transplantations are also performed in coordination with the Urology department for comprehensive management of end-stage kidney disease.
  • Oncology & Immunology: Specialized medical oncologists and immunologists at Sri Dwarakamayee deliver targeted treatments for various cancers and immune system disorders. Patient-centric care and cutting-edge therapies guide each treatment plan.
  • Haematology: Our haematology services diagnose and manage conditions like aplastic anaemia and myelodysplastic syndromes. Advanced testing (cytogenetics, immunophenotyping) supports precise, individualized patient care.
  • Gastroenterology: We conduct endoscopic procedures, colonoscopies, and liver biopsies to diagnose and treat gastrointestinal diseases. Tailored management plans help restore digestive health and improve quality of life.
  • Neurology: Our neurologists employ tools like EMG, EEG, nerve conduction studies, CT scans, and MRI to diagnose and treat neurological conditions ranging from epilepsy and migraines to cerebrovascular disorders.
  • Respiratory Medicine: With a dedicated department for pulmonary conditions, we manage asthma, COPD, sleep apnoea, and other respiratory disorders. Skilled specialists provide both medical and minimally invasive treatment options.
  • Orthopaedics: Our comprehensive musculoskeletal care includes the diagnosis and treatment of fractures, sports injuries, and degenerative joint diseases. Collaboration among orthopaedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and pain management experts enhances recovery.
  • Obstetrics & Gynaecology: From routine prenatal check-ups to complex gynaecological surgeries, our team supports women at every stage of life. Services include fertility treatments, antenatal care, and personalized birthing plans.
  • Infectious Diseases: We provide expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of infectious diseases, ensuring effective management and prevention strategies.

Combining advanced technology with a patient-first ethos, Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals stands out as a leading general medicine hospital in Karimnagar.
