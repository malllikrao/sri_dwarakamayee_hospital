Understanding Our Physiotherapy Department

The Department of Physiotherapy at Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals aims to maximize human potential through a proactive approach to health and wellness. We offer a wide range of services and methods of treatment to musculoskeletal problems, neurological, cardio-thoracic, and pediatric problems ranging from general aches and pains to complex sports injuries. In addition to core competence in their required fields, our physiotherapists also have expertise and experience in advanced physiotherapy skills and techniques.

Preventive Rehabilitation:

Aim is to provide education, advice & interventions to prevent or slow onset of further impairments and maintain a person’s level of ability. Associated with decreased risk of injury and increased physical performance.

SPORTHO:

SPORTHO is a commitment by Sri Dwarakamayee Hospitals to serve the requirement of professional sporting fraternity & recreational sporting population, addresses their sports and fitness related injuries by injury prevention, adjusting postures, form and movement patterns to help reduce risk of experiencing an injury.

At SPORTHO, we are proud to house the finest and most competent band of Sports Medicine Surgeons, delivering world-class care. Our skilled, specialist physiotherapists have the knowledge, skillset and track record of managing everyone – from elite athletes to fitness enthusiasts. We have advanced rehabilitation services focusing on injury prevention, injury management and safe return to play.

SPORTHO rehabilitation protocols are individualized and follow principles of tissue healing and exercise sequencing. Scientific monitoring of healing responses and criteria-based progression of activity levels make our rehabilitation programs safe and effective in preventing re-injuries and enabling return to play at competitive levels.

We specialize in movement assessment and retraining, focusing on sports-specific injury risk stratification and effective performance. Our exercise programs are unique and functionally oriented, which are developed with a deep understanding of sports-specific movements and the physiological load of a sport on a joint, muscle or the body, offering expertise in areas such as:

  • Movement Analysis & Movement Diagnosis
  • Evidence-based Injury Rehabilitation
  • Sports-specific Movement Retraining
  • Return to Competitive Play
  • Peak Athletic Performance

Strength & Conditioning:

Result-oriented fitness and coaching principles that focus on:

  • Stability, endurance and strength
  • Optimum power and plyometric training for explosiveness in sport performance
  • Weight / fat loss and muscle-building exercise programs
  • Young athlete-specific exercise programs
  • Posture correction and core stability
  • Recovery / prevention of re-injury
