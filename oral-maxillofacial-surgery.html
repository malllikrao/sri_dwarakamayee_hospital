Understanding Our Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Department

Maxillofacial Surgery and Dental Surgery treat conditions related to the face, jaw, and teeth. They cover procedures like oral maxillofacial surgery and dental reconstructions.

Highly skilled maxillofacial surgeons perform such surgeries that improve both function and appearance, ensuring optimal patient outcomes. These surgeries are essential for treating trauma, infections, and other complex oral issues.
