Meet Our Director: Dr. P. Vamshi Krishna

Dr. P. Vamshi Krishna, M.D. (General Medicine)

General Physician (Director & Main Doctor)

Dr. P. Vamshi Krishna is the visionary Director and a leading General Physician at Sri Dwarakamaye Hospitals. With extensive experience and a deep understanding of patient needs, Dr. Vamshi Krishna is at the forefront of our commitment to delivering exceptional healthcare. His leadership ensures that the hospital operates with the highest standards of medical excellence and ethical practice.

His expertise as an MD in General Medicine allows him to provide comprehensive primary care, focusing on accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and preventive health strategies. Dr. Vamshi Krishna has over 10+ years of experience in treating a wide range of medical conditions, ensuring top-notch care for all patients. His patient-first philosophy is the cornerstone of Sri Dwarakamaye Hospitals, inspiring the entire team to offer empathetic and holistic care.

Under his guidance, Sri Dwarakamaye Hospitals continues to innovate and expand its services, always with the goal of improving patient outcomes and contributing significantly to the health and well-being of the Karimnagar community.
